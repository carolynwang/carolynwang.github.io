01.

Software
engineer

After graduating [virtually] from UC Berkeley, I started working as a software development engineer at Amazon Web Services on the SageMaker data parallel team, developing SageMaker's distributed data parallelism library for deep learning training jobs on SageMaker. Prior to this, I interned on another AWS deep engines teams, during which I designed and implemented the initial release of SageMaker components for Kubeflow Pipelines. I also interned at MongoDB for a summer, designing and implementing support for facets in Atlas Search. As an engineer, I am passionate about designing and building accessible and high-impact products and love to tackle hard problems that require creative solutions.

02.

Photo
-grapher

I like taking people photos, and have since 2016. "Graduates", "Portraits", and "Street" are taken with a Canon R6 + 35mm 1.8 lens. I'm also partial to things that are just pleasing to the eye, so those photos live under "Places and things" and are taken with an iPhone.

In March 2022, I picked up a Minolta X-700 film camera and have started shooting on 35mm film, which has been fun and new! Those photos can be found in the "Film" section.

I'm always looking for people to do creative shoots with, so hmu if you're interested!
